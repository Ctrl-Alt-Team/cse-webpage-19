Welcome to this years Awesome Hackathon

So often we avoid running water, and running water is a lot of fun. We can fix anything. We have all at one time or another mixed some mud. Look around, look at what we have. Beauty is everywhere, you only have to look to see it. Even the worst thing we can do here is good. Even trees need a friend. We all need friends.

Every single thing in the world has its own personality - and it is up to you to make friends with the little rascals. Van Dyke Brown is a very nice brown, it's almost like a chocolate brown. Let's make some happy little clouds in our world. Let's go up in here, and start having some fun

The Organizers


I guess I'm a little weird. I like to talk to trees and animals. That's okay though; I have more fun than most people. Now let's put some happy little clouds in here. You're meant to have fun in life. We don't really know where this goes - and I'm not sure we really care. We'll do another happy little painting.
